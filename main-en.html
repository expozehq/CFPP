Briefly about us

Khmelnytskyi Charitable Foundation "Podilska Perlyna" was established on February 12, 2008 in order to carry out selfless charitable activities in the interests of society and provide assistance to people in need.
Since the beginning of its activities, the Foundation has focused on areas of work, namely:
• home care for the disabled and the elderly;
• providing humanitarian aid in the form of clothing, footwear, food and medicine - the "Caravan of Mercy" program;
• sewing workshop for sewing clothes;
• excursions to historical museums in Ukraine;
• organization of competitions for gifted children, orphans and large families;
• author's radio programs, publishing books for children;
• organization and participation in various competitions in Ukraine and abroad;
• legal and informational consultations;
• group training to provide assistance to students of educational institutions and young professionals.
